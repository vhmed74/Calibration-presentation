10. Troubleshooting & Error Resolution

Common Error Likely Root Cause Resolution Strategy
Slope Error Electrode aging or protein buildup. Perform deproteinization; check slope history; replace if < 45 mV.
Aspiration Failure Loose fluidic fittings or worn tubing. Tighten all connections; replace pump tubing; check probe for clots.
Voltage Drift Temperature shifts or air bubbles. Recalibrate system; check for fluidic leaks; verify grounding integrity.
QC Out of Range Expired controls or calibration shift. Rerun with fresh vial; perform 2-point calibration; check L-J trends.

Technical Advisory: Always follow a "simple-to-complex" logic. Start with basic fluidic and electrical checks before replacing expensive electrodes. If issues persist, consult authorized technical support.
