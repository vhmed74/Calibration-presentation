3. ISE Technology & Physics

The Roche 9180 utilizes highly sensitive modular electrodes. Each electrode is specifically engineered to respond to the electrical potential of a target ion.

The system uses the Nernst Equation to translate the measured potential into precise ionic concentrations for Na+, K+, Cl-, Ca2+, and Li+.

Roche's proprietary liquid-membrane technology ensures minimal interference and high durability.
