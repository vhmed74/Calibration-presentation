8. Quality Control Protocols

QC Method Frequency Clinical Objective
Internal QC Every 24 Hours Detect immediate random or systematic errors using standardized controls.
L-J Charts Continuous Visual tracking of QC results to identify shifts (sudden) or trends (gradual).
EQA / PT Quarterly External Proficiency Testing to compare lab accuracy against global peer groups.

Critical Westgard Rules

Rule 1-2s (Warning) One result exceeds 2SD. Requires close monitoring and review.
Rule 1-3s (Rejection) One result exceeds 3SD. Indicates major random error. Stop testing.
Rule 2-2s (Rejection) Two consecutive results exceed 2SD. Systematic error. Recalibrate.
