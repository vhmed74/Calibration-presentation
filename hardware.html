4. Hardware Architecture

Roche 9180 Unit

A compact, semi-automated analyzer designed for high-precision electrolyte measurement in small to medium labs.

Snap-Pak Reagents

All-in-one reagent system containing calibrators and waste container for safe, touch-free handling.

Modular Electrodes

Individual ISE electrodes for Na+, K+, Cl-, Ca2+, and Li+ that can be replaced independently.

Built-in Printer

Integrated thermal printer for immediate documentation of results and calibration data.
