9. Routine Maintenance

Frequency Core Maintenance Tasks
DAILY Clean sample probe; Perform deproteinization; Verify reagent levels and waste status.
WEEKLY Deep clean fluidic manifold; Inspect pinch valves for wear; Exterior surface disinfection.
MONTHLY Replace peristaltic pump tubing; Inspect electrode contact pins; Grounding integrity check.

Consumables Lifecycle Management

Track the "on-board life" of electrodes (typically 6–12 months). Proactive replacement of pump tubing and reagent packs prevents fluidic failure and ensures consistent aspiration pressure.
